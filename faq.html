FAQ

Answers from the second unit.

Do you print and ship shirts yourselves?

Nope. Spreadshirt handles product printing, payment, and shipping. We handle the bad jokes and shirt ideas.

Why are some designs grouped as Funny, Geography, CTA, and Design?

Those are the main browsing buckets on this site so you can find your preferred flavor of nonsense quickly.

Can I request a custom shirt idea?

Yes. Send us your idea through the contact page. If it makes us laugh, it has a chance.

Are all shirts shown here available right now?

This storefront reads from our current inventory JSON. Product availability and options are still controlled by Spreadshirt.
